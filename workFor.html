OPPORTUNITIES IN THE EVENT INDUSTRY!

The event industry is buzzing with exciting opportunities for those looking to jump into a fast-paced and dynamic field. Positions are available across various roles, catering to a wide range of skills and experiences.

OUR VISION

At XS Event Staffing, we are a team built by the people, for the people. Our commitment to client satisfaction matches our dedication to fostering a positive and empowering workplace for our staff. We recognize and celebrate our employees' unique talents. We believe that the happy and valued team will make each event a success.

Putting our staff first ensures a motivated and fulfilled workforce that delivers exceptional results for our clients.

WHO WE ARE LOOKING FOR

We don’t define talent by age or appearance at XS Event Staffing. Instead, we value passion, expertise, and a dedication to delivering exceptional event experiences.

We’re searching for professionals with diverse skills in catering, hospitality, promotions, conventions, and event production. We want to hear from you if you have a knack for creating unforgettable moments and thriving in a dynamic environment.

XS WORK ETHICS

Every staff member at XS is an invaluable asset, bringing their unique skills and experience to the table. We believe that happy staff creates successful events, and we prioritize the well-being and satisfaction of our employees.

Our supportive environment fosters growth, collaboration, and a sense of belonging, ensuring that every team member feels valued and motivated to contribute to the company’s long-term success.

We're all about staying connected! Our goal is to keep our communication open and quick, and we’ll do our best to respond to your requests within an hour.

FLEXIBILITY

We understand the need for a healthy work-life balance, so we offer a flexible schedule. You decide when and how often you want to work without the pressure of a minimum availability requirement.

Our staff is the backbone of the company, and we are looking for people who will contribute their unique skills and foster a positive work environment in the long run.

COMPETITIVE RATES

As a small agency, we keep operational costs low while maintaining a highly efficient workforce. This allows us to offer rates that are above the industry average.

We understand that every event is unique and comes with its own set of challenges and requirements. That’s why we offer flexible rates to our clients and therefore to our staff to be able to accommodate the varying complexities of the services our event staff provides.

This approach helps us attract more business from clients, provide more work for our team, and retain top talent in the competitive event industry.

Workers’ Compensation

We prioritize your safety and peace of mind by providing workers’ compensation. In the event of a job-related injury or illness, you’ll receive the medical care you need without the burden of out-of-pocket expenses. Additionally, workers’ comp offers wage replacement, allowing you to recover without financial stress.

Flexible Tax Options

At XS Event Staffing, we offer the flexibility of choosing between W2 or W9 options. This ensures you can handle your taxes in the way that suits you best. This personalized approach not only empowers you but also fosters a more tailored and rewarding work experience.

TRAVEL, PARKING, FOOD COMPENSATION

We recognize the logistical challenges of working in the event industry, especially in Los Angeles. That’s why we go the extra mile to support our staff:

  • Parking Compensation: We reimburse parking expenses for all events. If you use Uber or Lyft, your ride costs will be covered within the designated parking budget.
  • Gas Allowance: For event locations requiring more than an hour’s travel from downtown, we provide a gas allowance to ease the burden of commuting.
  • Food Allowance: For events lasting over six hours where the client does not provide food, we aim to provide an allowance to keep you energized and focused.

Join the XS Team

At XS Event Staffing, we’re committed to providing a supportive and rewarding work environment for individuals passionate about the event industry. From competitive pay rates to unparalleled flexibility, we ensure our team members feel valued and motivated.

If you’re ready to bring your skills to an industry-leading team, join XS Event Staffing today!
